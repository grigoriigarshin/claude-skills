Ghost
Chats

We're making agents more capable and assigning them smarter. But none of that helps if a big chunk of contacts aren't even real.

A customer opens a chat, gets connected to an agent, but never responds. The agent sits there for 4-6 minutes before they can close it.

~15%
of all global chats are ghosts
DISCOVERY
What happens in a ghost chat
What we need to find out
Root cause
Did the user even see they were connected? Is it a UX problem, spam, or something else?
Variation
Rates differ across entities and LoBs. Is it cultural, technical, or something in the product?
Downstream effect
Do ghost chats lead to re-contacts? Do they hurt retention or CSAT? Related to spam and fraud?
