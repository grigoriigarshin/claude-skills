What it takes to go live in a new entity

Identifying and closing feature gaps

  • Audit the entity's flows against CFX capabilities to find what's missing
  • Gaps are scoped, prioritised, and built — often requiring work on both sides

Template migration and QA

  • Entity teams rebuild their flows in CFX format — automation logic, content, edge cases
  • QA is run jointly with the entity's ops and product teams, iterating on each template together

Operational alignment

  • Agreeing on which flows to migrate first, how to measure success, and what the go/no-go criteria are
  • Aligning rollout timing with the entity's business calendar and central roadmap priorities
